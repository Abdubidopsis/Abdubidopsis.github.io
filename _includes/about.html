About

I am a PhD DAAD fellow at the University of Cologne. Here on this page I share all my achievements, publications, portofolio and other research related material produced and generated by me. My research centers on unraveling the physiological and genetic responses of endangered Arabis floodplain species to abiotic stresses.

With expertise in Plant molecular biology, and bioinformatics, I specialize in analyzing large-scale datasets to uncover the intricate molecular mechanisms that drive plant adaptation in challenging environments.!
